HAWA MAHAL

A blend of beauty and Rajasthan culture, the Hawa Mahal also known as "Palace of Winds" is situated in the capital of Rajasthan, Jaipur. Built from red and pink sandstones by the Maharaja Sawi Pratap Singh in 1799, this unique five storey structure is one of the most prominent tourist attractions in the Jaipur city.

What is the famous thing about Hawa Mahal?

The Hawa Mahal is a five-storey building, and it is the tallest building in the world that has been built without a foundation. It has a curved architecture that leans at an 87 degree angle, and a pyramidal shape which has helped it stay erect for centuries.
